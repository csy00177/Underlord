Underlord has two divison of Synergies. First one is "Race" and Second one is "Type"
"Race" is what 'defines' the heros. For example, Lycan, Dragon Knight, Cristal Maden are Humans.
On the other hand, "Type" represents what heros can do. In another word, it indicates what their 'job' is.
Using these two types of Synergies, players have to find the most efficient and powerful combinations to fight other players and achieve higher rank.

Each synergies have their own identities and powerfulness but
the result can be changed significantly even with small changes in combinations.
